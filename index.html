Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba (鬼滅の刃) is a Manga and Anime series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge and adapted by Ufotable studios respectively. It follows teenage Tanjiro Kamado, who joins the Demon Slayer Corps after his family is slaughtered and the sole survivor, his younger sister Nezuko, is turned into a demon, in the hopes of turning her human again and defeating the demon king Muzan Kibutsuji
